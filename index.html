FAQ

Everything you need to know before ordering

Ordering
How do I place an order?▼
Click "Order via WhatsApp" on any kit, or message us directly at @jerseyzone_za or 071 039 6374. Tell us the kit name, your size, and the type (Imported or Replica). Once payment is confirmed your order is secured.
How long does delivery take?▼
Imported kits ship internationally and arrive in 17–21 days. Ordering multiple items together gets you a discount since it reduces international shipping costs.
Can you source a kit not in the catalogue?▼
Yes. DM us a picture of the kit and we'll try to source it. Please note this may have additional fees and a longer lead time.
Kit Types
Player vs Fan version — what's the difference?▼
Player Version: High-performance lightweight materials, moisture-wicking technology, slim athletic fit, heat-pressed badges.

Fan Version: Durable comfortable materials, relaxed fit, embroidered/stitched badges — great for everyday wear at a lower price point.
What does REP and 🔵 mean in listings?▼
To avoid Instagram's word filtering: REP = Replica, 🔵 = Imported (Player/Fan or Retro). This protects our account — the products are exactly as described.
Do Replica kits have customisation add-ons?▼
No. Replica kits do not include add-ons. All imported kits offer custom name & number plus selected badges. Some imported kits also offer long sleeve — noted in each listing.
